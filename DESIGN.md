# Design System: Nordic Financial Light

## Aesthetic
- **Style:** Clean, professional, "Nordic Financial Light". High-end SaaS feel.
- **Layout:** High-density grids, clear data visualization.
- **Vibe:** Trustworthy, efficient, modern.

## Colors
- **Primary:** Dark Navy / Midnight Blue (Professional, Trust).
- **Background:** White / Light Gray (#F5F7FA) for content areas.
- **Status Indicators:**
  - 🟢 **Green:** Activity scheduled / Good standing.
  - 🟡 **Yellow:** Recent contact (<4 weeks), needs attention.
  - 🔴 **Red:** No contact >4 weeks, critical.

## Typography
- **Font Family:** Inter or Roboto (Clean sans-serif).
- **Headings:** Bold, dark slate.
- **Body:** Readable gray for data inputs.

## Components
- **Tables:** Dense data tables with zebra striping or subtle borders.
- **Cards:** White cards with subtle shadows for "Company Intelligence" items.
- **Navigation:** Sidebar navigation for easy access to modules.
