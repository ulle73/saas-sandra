# Design System: Lösen
**Project ID:** 12356634150435692596

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The "Nordic Financial Light" aesthetic: Clean, professional, and trustworthy. Focuses on high legibility, ample whitespace, and a sophisticated color palette that feels modern yet reliable. Dense data is handled with clarity through subtle borders and deliberate vertical rhythm.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
- **Pure Canvas** (#FFFFFF) – Main background and page surfaces.
- **Deep Slate** (#0F172A) – Primary branding, headings, and high-impact CTAs.
- **Financial Mint** (#10B981) – Positive associations: growth, scheduled activity, "Green" status.
- **Alert Amber** (#F59E0B) – Attention required: "Yellow" status, warnings.
- **Urgent Coral** (#EF4444) – Immediate action: "Red" status, missed activities.
- **Cool Gray** (#64748B) – Secondary text, icons, and subtle borders.

## 3. Typography Rules
- **Font Family:** Clean sans-serif (Inter, Roboto, or SF Pro).
- **Headings:** Deep Slate, Font-weight 600-700, slightly reduced letter spacing.
- **Body:** Cool Gray for metadata, Deep Slate for primary information, Font-weight 400-500.

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Buttons:** 8px rounded corners, solid background for primary, ghost/border for secondary.
* **Cards/Rows:** Subtle 1px borders (#E2E8F0), no shadows unless hovered (soft 4px blur).
* **Status Indicators:** Pills with soft backgrounds and high-contrast text.

## 5. Layout Principles
- **Grid:** Strict 4px/8px baseline grid.
- **Margins:** Generous (24px-32px) between sections.
- **Sidebar:** Collapsible, dark background or clean white with subtle separation.
