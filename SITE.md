## 2. Vision
Build a clean, professional sales-intelligence SaaS that helps account teams:
- Track contacts and companies
- See status and next action at a glance
- Review AI-assisted lead opportunities
- Coordinate weekly follow-ups

## 3. Non-Negotiable UX Direction
- Keep every page simple and easy to scan.
- Prioritize clarity over visual complexity.
- Keep a professional, trustworthy SaaS style.
- Every generated page must also produce one screenshot image (`queue/{page}.png`).
