# Project: Lösen (SaaS Sandra)

Project ID: 4465511803552248448

## Vision
To build **Lösen** – a sales‑intelligence SaaS that aggregates contacts, tracks interactions, colours status, monitors company news, and provides a weekly AI‑generated outreach list.
Target users are salespeople and account managers comprising a single dashboard for relationship management.

## Sitemap
- **Dashboard (/):** The main view. Contact list with status indicators (Green/Yellow/Red).
- **Company Intelligence (/intelligence):** News feed with keyword detection (layoffs, varsel, etc.).
- **Weekly Leads (/leads):** AI-generated list of high-priority prospects with pitch snippets.
- **Login (/login):** Authentication screen.

## Roadmap
1. **MVP Phase**:
   - Contact Dashboard with status logic.
   - Company Intelligence news feed.
   - Weekly AI Assistant for leads.
   - User Authentication.
