A "Company Intelligence" view for Lösen that visualizes real-time news alerts and trend analysis.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, Desktop-first
- Theme: Light, Nordic Financial (Clean, high-legibility)
- Background: Pure White (#FFFFFF)
- Primary Accent: Deep Slate (#0F172A)
- Support Colors: Mint (#10B981) for positive news, Coral (#EF4444) for negative alerts (varsel, layoffs), Cool Gray (#64748B) for general sentiment.
- Font: Clean modern sans-serif
- Layout: Structured grid, generous whitespace, 8px corner radii
- Style: Professional, data-driven, insightful

**Page Structure:**
1. **Sidebar:** Persistent navigation (Intelligence active).
2. **Header:** Search and Filter bar (Filter by company or keyword).
3. **Dashboard Content:**
   - **News Feed:** Cards with title, source, timestamp, and a sentiment/type indicator.
   - **Alert Panel:** Side panel showing detected keywords (e.g., "Varsel", "Order").
   - **Trend Chart:** Simple visualization of news volume or sentiment over the last 7 days.
4. **Alerts Toggle:** Settings for Telegram/Email notifications.
