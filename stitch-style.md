# Losen CRM - Feature Brief For Styling AI

## Design goal (highest priority)
The product must feel simple, structured, and fast to scan. Users should get a strong overview immediately and always understand what needs action next.

Core UX principles:
1. Clear hierarchy first.
2. Overview before detail.
3. Very low cognitive load.
4. Fast action paths.
5. Consistent status language and visual patterns.

## Styling direction
1. Make overview blocks dominant and instantly readable.
2. Use clean spacing and clear grouping.
3. Keep tables/lists highly scannable.
4. Keep selected/active states clear but not visually noisy.
5. Keep interactions professional and subtle.
6. Keep the entire experience focused on "overview first, action second, detail third".
